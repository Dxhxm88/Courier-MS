FAQ


Q: How can I search Parcel?

A: You need to enter your parcel tracking number in TRACK & TRACE and click 'Submit'. If you forgot your parcel tracking number, you also can ask admin for 'List Parcel'.

Q: Where can I pick up my parcel?

A: At Admin Office near Main Lobby . Pick up only can made at 2.30PM.

Q: How can I contact person in charge parcel?

A: Click 'Location & Parcel' for contact our staff.
