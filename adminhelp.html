FAQ


Q: How can I search Parcel?

A: Enter tracking parcel in "Search Parcel' and click 'Submit'.

Q: How can I update data?

A: Click 'Menu' and choose 'Update' then insert tracking number that you want to update.

Q: How can I insert new parcel?

A: Click 'Menu' and choose 'Add new parcel.

Q: How can I print all data?

A: Click 'View All' at below of 'Search Parcel' and click 'Print'.
